about

2K12CR is a comedic multi-genre band from Austin, TX comprised of Ben Cloutier (Vocals / Production) & David Menendez (Guitar / Bass). Started by Ben in 2012 as a solo act, 2K12CR released it's first album entitled This is A Very Good Album of Rap Music in 2014. In 2015 David teamed up with Ben and released the second album Nothing 2 Lose. In 2016 production began on 2K12CR's third release Very Good Bad Boys and is set for release in early 2017.

With music spanning various genres and comedic lyrical content 2K12CR has found a niche in the Austin music scene and has started gathering a sizeable local following. We have big plans and will continue to produce music and play venues around Austin. With no shortage of music or ideas you can expect lots in the future from 2K12CR.
